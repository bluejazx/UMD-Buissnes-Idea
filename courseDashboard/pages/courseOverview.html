Financial Awareness Course

Course Goals

This course is designed for college students and will be presented at the UMD Pitch Dingman competition through the Robert H. Smith School of Business. It aims to:

  • Instill basic financial knowledge
  • Introduce practical budgeting skills
  • Develop financial awareness
