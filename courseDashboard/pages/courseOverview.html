Welcome to Your Financial Journey

This course aims to instill basic financial knowledge, introduce practical budgeting skills, and develop financial awareness. Navigate through the weeks to build your financial foundation step by step

About the UMD Pitch Dingman Competition

This course is designed with college students in mind and is being pitched at the UMD Pitch Dingman competition, hosted by the Robert H. Smith School of Business. The competition provides an opportunity to showcase innovative educational solutions aimed at empowering students with financial literacy and practical budgeting skills.
